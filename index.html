FAQ & Terms

What is Sayali Rental Closet?

+
Sayali Rental Closet offers a curated selection of bridal, non-bridal, maternity, festive, and designer wear for women. Rent, Slay, Return, Repeat without the high cost of ownership.

What items are available for rent?

+
Bridal lehengas, sarees (Nauvari, designer, silk), gowns, maternity photoshoot outfits, kids' outfits, and jewelry on select packages.

What is the rental period?

+
Standard rental duration is 3 days. Extended rentals can be arranged with additional charges.

Do you provide trial or fitting?

+
Yes, book a free trial appointment at our Pune shop to try outfits before finalizing.

Do you alter the dress to fit?

+
We offer minor, temporary alterations for a perfect fit. Major changes are not provided.

What are the rental charges?

+
Charges vary by outfit, design, and duration. Contact us or visit our shop for a custom quote.

Is a security deposit required?

+
Yes, a refundable deposit is collected at booking, returned after outfit inspection.

How do I return the outfit?

+
Return in the same condition within the agreed period. Late returns incur extra charges.

What if I damage the dress?

+
Minor damages may incur repair fees. Major damages or loss may require full payment of the outfit’s value.

Can I cancel my booking?

+
Cancellations: 48+ hours before pickup (full refund), within 48 hours (50% refund), on pickup day or later (no refund).
Terms & Conditions

1. Booking & Payment

+
Full rental payment and deposit are required in advance via cash, UPI, or bank transfer.

2. Use & Care

+
Use outfits with care. Avoid makeup stains, burns, or cuts. Do not wash or dry clean rented items.

3. Returns & Damages

+
Return by the due date. Damages are assessed, and repair/replacement costs are deducted from the deposit. Lost items incur full market value charges.

4. Fitting & Alterations

+
Minor fitting adjustments are offered during trial. Renters must not alter outfits.

5. Late Returns

+
₹500 per day for late returns. Non-returned items after 5 days without communication are considered lost, incurring full value charges.

6. Hygiene & Maintenance

+
All outfits are professionally cleaned after use. Renters must not wash or alter cleaning.

7. Refunds & Cancellations

+
Refunds follow the cancellation policy in the FAQ. Processing takes 3–5 working days.

8. Photography & Branding

+
With consent, customer photos may be used for marketing. Tag us in your pictures!

9. Legal

+
Disputes are subject to Pune, Maharashtra jurisdiction. We reserve the right to refuse service for misuse.
