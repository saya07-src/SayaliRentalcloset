FAQ & Terms

Q: What is Sayali Rental Closet?

+
Sayali Rental Closet is a fashion rental service offering a wide range of bridal, non-bridal, maternity, festive, and designer wear on rent for women. Our goal is to help you Rent, Slay, Return, Repeat without the high cost of ownership.

Q: What items are available for rent?

+
We offer: Bridal lehengas; Sarees (including Nauvari, Designer, Silk); Gowns & dresses; Maternity photoshoot outfits; Kids outfits; Jewelry (on select packages).

Q: What is the rental period?

+
Standard rental duration is 3 days. Extended rental can be arranged on request with extra charges.

Q: Do you provide trial or fitting before booking?

+
Yes! You can book a free trial appointment at our shop for fittings and to try different outfits before finalizing.

Q: Do you alter the dress to fit?

+
We offer temporary alterations (minor size adjustments) to make the dress fit perfectly. Major size changes are not provided.

Q: What are the rental charges?

+
Charges vary depending on the outfit, design, and duration. Please contact us or visit the shop for a price list or custom quotation.

Q: Is a security deposit required?

+
Yes. A refundable security deposit is collected at the time of booking. This is returned after outfit inspection upon return.

Q: How do I return the outfit?

+
Outfits should be returned in the same condition within the agreed rental duration. Late return will incur extra charges.

Q: What happens if I damage the dress?

+
Minor damages (e.g., loose thread, stains) may incur repair charges. Major damages or unreturnable items may require full payment of the product value.

Q: Can I cancel my booking?

+
Cancellations made: 48 hours before pickup: Full refund. Within 48 hours: 50% refund. On the pickup day or later: No refund.
Terms & Conditions (English)

1. Booking & Payment

+
Full rental payment and security deposit are required in advance to confirm your booking. Payments can be made via cash, UPI, or bank transfer.

2. Use & Care

+
Outfits must be used with care. Avoid harsh makeup stains, burns, and cuts. Do not attempt to wash or dry clean rented clothes.

3. Returns & Damages

+
Return must be made on or before the return date. Any damage will be assessed, and repair or replacement cost will be deducted from the deposit. If the dress is permanently damaged or lost, full market value will be charged.

4. Fitting & Alterations

+
Only minor fitting changes are offered and must be discussed during trial. Clothes must be returned without any alterations done by the renter.

5. Late Returns

+
₹___ per day will be charged for each day of late return. If not returned within 5 days without communication, it will be treated as lost and full value will be claimed.

6. Hygiene & Maintenance

+
All outfits are professionally cleaned after each use. You are not allowed to wash or alter cleaning in any way.

7. Refunds & Cancellations

+
Refunds are processed as per cancellation policy stated in FAQ. Refunds may take 3–5 working days.

8. Photography & Branding

+
We may request customer photos (with your consent) for marketing or social media. Tag us in your beautiful pictures!

9. Legal

+
Any disputes will be subject to jurisdiction under Pune, Maharashtra. Sayali Rental Closet reserves the right to refuse service to anyone found misusing the service.
